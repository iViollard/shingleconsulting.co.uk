Gary Wigrove FRICS

Gary has significant experience in working for corporate end users, consulting and delivering a range of workplace and property strategies.

  • Workplace design
  • Senior stakeholder management
  • Real estate strategy
  • Programme and project management
  • Commercial management
  • Multi-site roll outs
  • Migration strategy
  • Employer’s agent
  • Procurement strategy
  • Data centre and operational spaces

Gary has 40 years’ experience in the construction industry initially working for both major and specialist fit out contractors and the last 20 years working for major corporates including, BT, UBS and Morgan Stanley, developing strategies and then delivering a variety of programmes and projects including new and refurbished corporate HQ’s, data centres, national relocation projects and rebranding, he has led both in house and external project teams and has reported at Board level.

His knowledge and passion for the corporate workplace has led him to be involved in a number of industry bodies, he is a past president of the BCO, former trustee at the BRE, on the judging panel for the EG Awards and a regular speaker and panel member at industry events.
